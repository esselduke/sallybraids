Policies

Please review our policies before booking your appointment.

A 35% non-refundable deposit is required to secure all appointments.

  • Deposits can be transferred to a future appointment with at least 48 hours notice
  • Same-day cancellations or no-shows forfeit the deposit
  • Remaining balance is due in full at the end of your appointment
  • We accept cash, credit, debit, and e-transfer

We value your time and ours. Please arrive on time for your appointment.

  • Late arrivals (15+ minutes) may result in shortened service time or rescheduling
  • No-shows will forfeit their deposit and may be required to pay in full upfront for future bookings
  • If you're running late, please call us at +1 (514) 969-7169

To ensure we can complete your service on time, certain styles have booking cutoff times:

  • Small Knotless Braids: Do NOT book after 7:00 AM (8.5+ hour service)
  • Bohemian & Tribal Styles: Do NOT book after 8:00 AM (7-8.5 hour service)
  • Men's Pop Smoke & Straightbacks: Do NOT book after 4:00 PM (2.5-3 hour service)

These restrictions ensure we can provide quality service without rushing. Our hours are 7 AM – 7 PM daily.

Hair extensions and materials are NOT included in most service prices unless explicitly stated.

  • Please review each service description for specific hair requirements
  • For services where hair is NOT provided, you must bring your own extensions or purchase them from us
  • We recommend high-quality braiding hair for best results and longevity
  • Beads and other decorative accessories are typically not included

We love to showcase our work and celebrate your beauty!

  • By booking with us, you consent to photos being taken during and after your appointment
  • Photos may be used on our social media, website, and promotional materials
  • If you prefer not to be photographed or featured, please let us know before your appointment
  • We respect your privacy and will honor all opt-out requests
